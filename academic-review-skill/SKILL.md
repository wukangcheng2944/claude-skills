## Agent-First Output Rule

This skill should not stop at a general review summary.
It should help the next agent decide what to revise first, what evidence matters most, and which section or citation should be inspected next.

When the review will be handed off, end with:
- `suspected_scope`
- `strongest_evidence`
- `next_best_action`
- `next_target`
- `confidence`

`next_target` should be as concrete as possible: a chapter, section, paragraph, figure, citation block, or structure node.
